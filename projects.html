Projects

What your donations actually do

Six focused programmes that together address the most immediate needs of the families and communities we work with.

Education sponsorship

From just £20 a month you can sponsor a student’s entire journey through school, and on to vocational training, college or university. We cover fees, uniform, books and everything else they need.

Sponsor a student →

Medical fund

Donations to our medical fund cover emergency and routine treatment for people who would otherwise go without. You can also sponsor the ongoing care of someone living with a long-term condition like sickle cell, epilepsy or diabetes.

Rice appeal

Our rice fund delivers food to families and community groups in acute crisis. £25 provides a 25kg bag; £40 provides a 50kg bag. Last year we delivered more than five tonnes.

Reading groups

Small reading groups that help students improve their literacy and, we hope, fall in love with books. We welcome donations of children’s and teenage books to stock our shelves.

Spoken English sessions

Conversational English practice that gives students confidence and fluency, helping them unlock opportunities in further education and work.

Sexual health education

Open, age-appropriate sessions for young adults on relationships and sexual health, run in partnership with local medical professionals. We also help arrange STI testing and treatment where it’s needed.

Current campaign

Light Up Salone · solar power for four villages

Our biggest campaign yet, marking our 10-year anniversary. We’re raising £30,000 to install solar streetlights in four rural villages, bringing light, safety and opportunity after sundown.
