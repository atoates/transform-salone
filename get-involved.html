Get involved

Four ways to make a difference

Whether you have £5 or five weeks to give, there’s a way to be part of this.

Sponsor a student

From just £20 a month you can sponsor a child’s education all the way through school and on to further study. Your monthly gift covers fees, uniform, books, pens, the lot. Many of our sponsored children have gone on to become teachers, nurses and trades people in their communities.

Become a sponsor

Sponsor medical care

You can also sponsor ongoing medical care for a child or adult living with a long-term condition, or help support a vulnerable baby whose family is struggling. Get in touch and we’ll tell you more about who needs help right now.

Ask for details

Fundraise for us

Run a marathon. Hold a bake sale. Climb a mountain. Jump out of a plane. Whatever you enjoy, we can set you up with a JustGiving or Wonderful.org page and cheer you on. Fundraisers keep us going, and they often turn into lifelong supporters.

Start a fundraiser

Volunteer in Sierra Leone

Come and see the work for yourself. Our trips are small, hands-on and, by every account, life changing. You’ll spend time with the students we support and get to know the communities we work in.

Enquire about trips

Prefer to give directly?

You can make a one-off or regular donation by card, PayPal, bank transfer, cheque or standing order. Every penny goes to our projects in Sierra Leone.

See donation options

A UK-registered charity working with communities in Sierra Leone. Providing education, medical care and food relief since 2014.
