What we do

Small charity, deep roots

Our work is focused and hands-on. Every penny donated goes directly to the projects you see below, managed by a team on the ground in Sierra Leone.

Education that lasts a lifetime

Sponsorships and scholarships that see students through primary school, secondary school and on to vocational training, college or university. Many of our sponsored students have gone on to become teachers, nurses and trades people in their own communities.

Medical care

Emergency treatment for those who cannot afford it, and ongoing care for people living with long-term conditions.

Food relief

Rice distribution for families and groups struggling in times of crisis. Over five tonnes delivered last year.

Light Up Salone

Our 10-year campaign: solar power for rural villages

Nearly nine in ten people in Sierra Leone live without electricity. When the sun sets, daily life stops. We’re raising funds to install solar streetlights in four rural villages, so children can study, traders can work later, and women and girls can walk home safely.
