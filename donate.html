Every pound goes to Sierra Leone

We’re all volunteers. No salaries, no offices, no fundraising overheads. Your gift, big or small, lands where it’s needed.

Where it goes

Scroll to see what your gift changes

Every amount, big or small, reaches a real family. Here’s what a single donation buys on the ground.

£20

A month of school fees

Covers fees, books and uniform for one sponsored student for a month.

£20

A month of school

Sponsor a child’s fees, books and uniform for one month. Multiply that by twelve and you have put a child through an entire school year, something many families in rural Sierra Leone simply cannot afford on their own.

£40

A 50kg bag of rice

Delivered to a family or community group in acute crisis. Last year supporters like you funded more than five tonnes of rice for households that had run out of food.

£100

A solar streetlight panel

Your contribution pays for a single panel in our Light Up Salone campaign. When the sun sets over rural Sierra Leone, life used to stop. Now children can study after dark and women and girls can walk home safely.

£500

A year of care

Funds ongoing medical treatment for someone living with a long-term condition like sickle cell, epilepsy or diabetes. For many, it is the difference between managing a condition and dying quietly from it.
