Every pound goes to Sierra Leone

We’re all volunteers. No salaries, no offices, no fundraising overheads. Your gift, big or small, lands where it’s needed.

  • JustGiving
  • PayPal

JustGiving

Give by card, set up Gift Aid, and share your support with friends and family.

JustGiving charges a small fee (1.9% + 20p per donation, and 5% of any Gift Aid claimed).

Donate via JustGiving

Wonderful.org

Give through your bank app. 100% of your donation reaches us; Wonderful.org covers all the fees.

Gift Aid is collected automatically and you’ll get an email confirmation.

Donate via Wonderful

PayPal

Donate via PayPal Giving Fund. No fees are charged to the charity or to you.

Donate via PayPal

Standing order & Gift Aid

Set up a regular monthly gift and, if you’re a UK taxpayer, add 25% at no cost to you through Gift Aid. Contact us and we’ll send you the forms.
