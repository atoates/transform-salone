Our story

Four friends, one shared love of Sierra Leone

Transform Salone grew out of friendships formed on volunteer trips. Today it’s a small, focused charity doing big things, quietly.

Transform Salone was founded in 2014 by four friends who had each spent time volunteering in Sierra Leone and found their lives changed by it. The charity was officially registered in England and Wales in July 2015.

What began as a handful of people raising money for the children and families they had come to care about has grown into a charity supporting long-term education, ongoing medical care, food relief in times of crisis, and community projects across rural Sierra Leone. We’ve stayed small on purpose. Every trustee is a volunteer, everyone knows the work personally, and every pound we raise goes straight to projects on the ground.

How we work

We run lean. There is no office, no salaries, no fundraising overhead. Decisions are made by trustees who know Sierra Leone first-hand and are in regular contact with Lamin, our volunteer country manager, who lives in Sierra Leone and oversees our day-to-day projects there.

That closeness matters. It means we can respond quickly when a family needs help paying for urgent medical treatment, or when a student’s school fees fall short, or when a village faces a food shortage. And it means supporters can see exactly where their money goes.

The team

Meet the trustees

Every member of our team gives their time for free. Here’s who we are.

AH

Founding trustee

First visited Sierra Leone in 2006 and has returned regularly ever since. Alison oversees Transform Salone’s projects and leads much of our UK awareness work.

RF

Ronnie Fleming

Founding trustee

Runs an executive coaching and management consultancy. Brings years of experience leading community development teams in Sierra Leone, Brazil, Moldova, Sri Lanka and beyond.

SO

Stephi Oates

Founding trustee

Has supported the education of seven young people in Sierra Leone, all of whom have now graduated. Works in her family’s building and development business.

HY

Helen Young

Trustee

Visited Sierra Leone in 2016 and became a trustee in 2018. Known for dreaming up creative fundraisers: mountain climbs, building jumps, cold-water swims.

LB

Lamin Bangura

Country manager, Sierra Leone

Our eyes and ears on the ground. Lamin started out as one of our sponsored students and now coordinates day-to-day projects across the country while studying economics.

CH

Carla Husband

Communications

Manages our social media and communications as a volunteer. First travelled to Sierra Leone in 2016 for a week, and has returned many times since.

In loving memory of Adele Pirrie

Founding trustee Adele Pirrie, affectionately known as Granny Granny, passed away on 20 April 2021. She first visited Sierra Leone in 2008 and felt it a privilege to serve the people she had come to love. She is deeply missed.
